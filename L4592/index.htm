Listener 4592

Graven Image by Phi

Theme notes

The three regions are columns 1-4, 5-8, 9-12. The mechanics in these regions are respectively:

  • reverse a word;
  • add a letter;
  • remove a letter.

These alterations are applied either to a word in the clue, or to the answer to give the entry. The odd ones out are 23D (KNIGHT, addition in reversal region), 28D (DEATH, subtraction in addition region) and 9D (DEVIL, reversal in subtraction region), thus referencing Albrecht Durer's incredibly famous engraving, hence the title: Graven Image. His name appears from the top left after a series of knight's moves (as in chess).
